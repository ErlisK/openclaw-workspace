## Chosen Niche: Commission Agreements for Indie Digital Artists

**Who:** Freelance digital artists / illustrators selling commissions (character art, logos, concept
art) via Twitter/X, DeviantArt, Etsy, Gumroad, or direct DMs.

**Why this niche wins (evidence summary):**

| Signal | Evidence |
|--------|----------|
| **Quote volume** | 19/58 quotes (33%) — highest cluster |
| **Frequency** | Weekly complaint threads on r/ArtistLounge, r/ArtBusiness |
| **Explicit demand** | "Does anybody have a commission contract template?" (r/ArtistLounge f6vwno) |
| **Root cause clarity** | Always: "no contract" or "bad template" |
| **Solution is obvious** | 7-question wizard → ready-to-sign PDF |
| **ROI on $9/year** | Protects $500–$5,000+/year in commissions |

## Initial Template Set

### T001 — Commission Agreement *(Launch, Core Wizard)*
- **Use case:** Artist ↔ client for any digital commission
- **Covers:** Work type, deliverable spec, revision policy, IP rights, payment terms, jurisdiction
- **Output:** 2–3 page plain-English PDF + document ID + embeddable badge
- **Wizard:** 7 questions (~90 seconds)
- **Pain solved:** Scope creep, unlimited revisions, ghosting, IP confusion

### T002 — Commercial License Add-on *(Launch, Toggle)*
- **Use case:** Buyer wants commercial rights (merch, advertising, resale) on top of a commission
- **Covers:** Territory, exclusivity, permitted uses, attribution requirements
- **Output:** 1-page addendum to T001
- **Wizard:** 2 additional questions on Q4 branch
- **Pain solved:** r/ArtistLounge (1ahi4ug) — buyers reselling commissions without rights

### T003 — Collaborator Revenue Split *(v1.1)*
- **Use case:** 2–4 creators splitting earnings on a joint project (game, zine, music release)
- **Covers:** % splits, vesting milestones, IP ownership, payout schedule, exit terms
- **Output:** 3-page agreement + revenue tracking spreadsheet template
- **Wizard:** 5 questions
- **Pain solved:** r/gamedev, gamedev.stackexchange — no contract before launch

### T004 — Asset License (Personal / Commercial) *(v1.1)*
- **Use case:** Creator selling digital assets, brushes, presets, fonts, UI packs
- **Covers:** Permitted uses, commercial/personal tiers, redistribution, attribution
- **Output:** 2-page license document embeddable in product listing
- **Wizard:** 5 questions
- **Pain solved:** itch.io forum — "commercial use" ambiguity driving buyers to competitors
