## Partner Value Proposition Summary

What we offer unions/training centers:

| Benefit | Details |
|---|---|
| **Paid reviewer gigs** | $25–$40/review for retired/active journeymen; flexible schedule |
| **Code standards input** | We build our badge taxonomy with their expertise; they get authorship credit |
| **Co-branded credentials** | "Verified by [Local X] × CertClip" badges carry union authority |
| **Graduate differentiation** | Their program graduates stand out in job market with verifiable credentials |
| **Data partnership** | Anonymized insights on which skills employers are searching for in their region |

What we need from them:

| Need | Details |
|---|---|
| **Mentor vetting** | 2–5 journeymen willing to do paid video reviews (initial pilot) |
| **Code standards input** | 2–3 hours of their Training Director's time to validate badge taxonomy |
| **Intro to graduates** | Permission to reach out to recent graduates for user interviews |
| **Credibility** | Public acknowledgment of the partnership for employer trust |
