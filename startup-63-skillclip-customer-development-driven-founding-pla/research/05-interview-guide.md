## Interview Structure (30 minutes)

### Opening (3 min)
- Brief intro: "I'm working on a product for the trades industry. I'm NOT selling anything — I want to understand your problems."
- Ask permission to record (if video call) or take notes

### Background (5 min)
1. Tell me about your role and what a typical week looks like.
2. How long have you been in the trades / hiring tradespeople?
3. How many people are on your team / how many hires do you make per year?

### Problem Discovery (15 min)
**For Employers/GCs:**
4. Walk me through the last time you hired a tradesperson — start to finish. What was the process?
5. What was the hardest part?
6. Have you ever had a worker who misrepresented their skills or credentials? What happened?
7. How do you verify someone's code compliance experience before they start?
8. Has a failed inspection ever been traced back to a worker's inexperience with local code? Tell me about that.
9. What would make you feel completely confident in a new hire before day 1?

**For Staffing Agencies:**
4. What's your first-week termination / send-back rate for skilled trade placements?
5. What's the most common reason for a send-back?
6. Walk me through how you currently verify a candidate's certifications.
7. How do you handle client audit requests for worker credentials?
8. If you could eliminate one step in your screening process, what would it be?

**For Tradespeople:**
4. Have you ever moved to a new state or city for work? How did you re-establish your credentials?
5. When was the last time you applied for a job and felt your skill level was underestimated or hard to prove?
6. How do you currently show a new employer what you're capable of?
7. Have you ever lost a job opportunity because of a credential or documentation issue?
8. If you could show an employer your best work in 2 minutes, what would that look like?

### Solution Exploration (5 min)
"I want to describe something we're working on and get your reaction — please be honest, including if it sounds useless."

[Describe CertClip in 60 seconds: video portfolio + jurisdiction badges + mentor verification]

9. What's your first reaction?
10. What's the biggest thing that would make you not trust this?
11. Who else in your organization would care about this?

### Wrap-up (2 min)
12. Is there anything else that's a bigger problem for you in hiring/credentialing that I haven't asked about?
13. Would you be willing to be an early user / advisor?
14. Who else should I talk to? (referrals)
