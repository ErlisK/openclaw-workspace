## Maker Comment

Hey Product Hunt! 👋

We built GrantPilot after watching small nonprofits spend $3,000–$8,000 per grant application — mostly on staff time for things a well-prompted LLM can do in minutes.

**What we built:**

🔍 **RFP Parser** — Upload any federal/state/foundation RFP PDF. Get structured requirements, deadlines, scoring rubric, eligibility checklist, and a Win Score in 90 seconds.

🏆 **Win Score** — 6-factor predictive score (eligibility match, narrative completeness, budget quality, compliance, org profile, QA review status) before you commit 40 hours.

✍️ **AI Narrative Builder** — 58 funder-specific templates + GPT-4 generation with org profile context. Matches your voice, hits word limits, covers all required sections.

💰 **Budget Builder** — OMB 2 CFR 200-compliant budget with personnel, fringe, indirect cost calculations and narrative justifications that reviewers expect.

📋 **Compliance Checklist** — Auto-generated from RFP requirements. Track completion, flag missing items before deadline.

👩‍💼 **Human QA Gate** — Every submission package gets 48-hour specialist review before export. Insurance-backed SLA.

**Who it's for**: Nonprofits ($50K–$5M budgets), neighborhood associations, municipal grant teams

**Free tier**: Parse unlimited RFPs, access 58 free templates, generate 1 Win Score per month.

Would love your feedback on the Win Score feature — it's the one thing interviewees said they wish existed.
