### Post 4 — Customer Discovery Finding (LinkedIn)
**Date:** 2025-04-10  
**Platform:** LinkedIn  

> We interviewed 47 nonprofit grant managers before writing a line of code. Here's what we learned:
> 
> 📍 The #1 pain point isn't writing — it's *knowing what to write*.
> 
> Nonprofits spend 20–40 hours per application just:
> - Parsing 80-page RFPs for what's actually required
> - Figuring out if they're even eligible before starting
> - Tracking which sections they've finished
> 
> That's before a single word of the narrative.
> 
> GrantPilot solves this first — parse any RFP in 90 seconds and get a structured outline, eligibility checklist, and Win Score before you commit your team's time.
