## Discovery Call Structure (30–45 min)

### Pre-call prep (5 min)
- Review prospect's org, role, recent content (LinkedIn, website)
- Identify likely pain point bucket (see segments below)
- Load case study most relevant to their segment
- Have pilot application link ready: citebundle.com/pilot

### Call agenda

**0–3 min: Open**
- "Thanks for making time. Quick agenda: I want to understand your workflow before I show you anything — 15 minutes of questions, then 15 minutes of demo if it's relevant. Sound good?"

**3–15 min: Discovery questions**

1. "Walk me through a typical piece of content your team produces — from brief to published."
2. "Where do citations and source verification fit in that workflow?"
3. "How long does it typically take from first draft to legal/client clearance?"
4. "Have you experimented with any AI writing tools? What happened?"
5. "What would need to be true for an AI tool to pass your compliance review?"
6. "If you could fix one thing about your current content production process, what would it be?"

**Pain point signals to listen for:**
- "Manual PubMed searching" → evidence graph value prop
- "Legal keeps sending things back" → compliance pre-check
- "Citations that don't support the claim" → Scite integration
- "We tried X but got burned" → non-consumer story, perfect fit
- "We need an audit trail" → compliance report export
- "Our clients want DOIs on everything" → citation bundle

**15–30 min: Demo (only if relevant pain confirmed)**
- Use their content type as framing: "Let me show you what this looks like for press releases / agency deliverables / journal abstracts"
- Show: claim extraction (1 min) → evidence match (2 min) → compliance flag (1 min) → citation bundle (1 min)
- Highlight the specific feature that maps to their pain

**30–35 min: Pilot offer**
- "Based on what you've told me, I think [Team/Agency/Enterprise] pilot makes sense."
- Show pricing: $1,500 / $3,000 / custom for 3 months
- "The pilot includes hands-on onboarding, weekly check-ins, and a full performance report at the end."
- "We have 3 pilot slots. Two are filled. Want me to send you the MoU?"

**35–40 min: Close / next steps**
- If warm: "I'll send the MoU today. It's a 2-page document, straightforward."
- If needs approval: "Who else needs to be in the room for this decision?"
- If not ready: "What would change your mind? What would you need to see?"
- Always: "Can I add you to our webinar list? We're doing a live demo next month."

## Prospect Segments + Tailored Positioning

### Segment A: Medical Writing Agencies
**Pain:** Revision cycles, citation disputes, losing hours to manual PubMed
**Hook:** "MedComm Solutions cut their legal clearance from 11 days to 4.5 days"
**Demo:** Press release → claim extraction → evidence graph → citation bundle
**Pilot tier:** Agency ($3,000/3 months)
**Objection:** "We have a process that works" → "This accelerates your existing process, it doesn't replace it"

### Segment B: Pharma Medical Affairs
**Pain:** Legal review bottleneck, compliance liability, audit trail for regulatory submissions
**Hook:** "BioInform Analytics verified 847 claims across 3 FDA advisory submissions in one quarter"
**Demo:** Clinical claim → Scite direction indicator → FDA 21 CFR 202 check → signed audit export
**Pilot tier:** Enterprise ($3,000+ / custom)
**Objection:** "We need security review first" → Send to citebundle.com/security, offer HECVAT

### Segment C: Health Journalism / Media
**Pain:** Pressure to publish fast while maintaining accuracy, no AI tools due to sourcing risk
**Hook:** "The reason journalists avoid AI tools isn't skepticism — it's that compliance teams aren't wrong"
**Demo:** Article draft → claim extraction → evidence graph → literacy-adapted output
**Pilot tier:** Pro ($149/mo, not pilot)
**Objection:** "Our org doesn't budget for this" → "One retraction costs more than a year of subscriptions"

### Segment D: Independent Medical Writers / AMWA/EMWA Members
**Pain:** Time-to-delivery pressure, CCMEP compliance, client expectations
**Hook:** "HealthWrite Agency doubled their content volume on the same team"
**Demo:** Client deliverable → citation bundle → compliance report
**Pilot tier:** Starter ($49/mo) or team pilot ($1,500/3 months)
**Objection:** "I'm solo, this feels expensive" → Calculator: at 2 hours saved per piece × 4 pieces/month = 8 hours

## Objection Handling

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|---------|
| "We already have a process" | "This accelerates your process — citation verification takes 2-4 hours, we do it in seconds. Your process still exists, it's just faster." |
| "We tried AI tools and got burned" | "That's exactly who we built this for. The problem wasn't AI — it was that general AI tools don't have an evidence layer. We do." |
| "Legal won't approve AI tools" | "Our design partner specifically said the audit trail is what convinced their legal team. They can see every source, every compliance check, every reviewer verdict." |
| "It's too expensive" | "What does one revision round cost your team in time? One retraction? The math usually works at $49/mo if you're doing 4+ pieces/month." |
| "We need security review" | "Absolutely. citebundle.com/security has our full security posture. We respond to HECVAT in 5 business days. HIPAA BAA available on Enterprise." |
| "Can we try before committing to a pilot?" | "Yes — 14-day free trial on all plans, no card required. Start at citebundle.com." |
| "Can you do [custom feature]?" | "Tell me more about what you need. Our design partners have direct input on the roadmap, and pilots come with roadmap inclusion." |

## Post-call Follow-up Sequence

**Within 2 hours:**
Send email with:
- "Nice to meet you" personalized note (1 sentence referencing something specific from call)
- Link to relevant case study
- MoU draft (if they expressed interest) or trial link
- Calendar link for next call

**Day 3 (if no reply to MoU):**
"Hi [Name], quick follow-up on the pilot MoU. Any questions on the terms? Happy to adjust the start date or scope."

**Day 7 (if still no reply):**
"Hi [Name], last follow-up — want to make sure you have what you need. If the timing isn't right, no problem. Happy to reconnect in [month]."
