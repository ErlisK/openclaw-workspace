## Key Findings

### The Blue Ocean
No competitor simultaneously scores high on **evidence rigor** AND **content production ease**. The upper-right quadrant of the Evidence Rigor vs. Content Production Ease map is **completely unoccupied**.

### The Market Bifurcation
- **AI writing tools** (Jasper, Copy.ai, ChatGPT): excellent content, zero evidence grounding, high hallucination risk
- **Research tools** (Scite, Consensus, Elicit): excellent evidence, zero content output pipeline
- **MLR compliance tools** (Veeva, Zinc): strong compliance, enterprise-only pricing, inaccessible to 95% of market

### Top 3 Pain Points Validated
1. AI tools hallucinate scientific claims with no accountability
2. Research tools produce outputs usable only by academics, not content teams  
3. MLR compliance is enterprise-only; mid-market uses email/Slack for compliance

### Four Actions (Summary)
- **Eliminate:** Generic marketing copy, social scheduling as product
- **Reduce:** Brand voice customization, social analytics
- **Raise:** Hallucination mitigation, provenance scoring, citation bundle quality
- **Create:** Claim-to-evidence pipeline automation, expert microtask marketplace, retraction monitoring, compliance agent at SMB pricing
