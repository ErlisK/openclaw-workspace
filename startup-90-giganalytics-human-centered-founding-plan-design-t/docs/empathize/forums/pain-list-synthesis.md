## GigAnalytics Feature Priority Map

Based on pain frequency × severity:

### Tier 1 (Must-Have MVP)
1. Multi-platform income import (CSV + PayPal/Stripe API)
2. True hourly rate calculation (net earnings ÷ hours after fees)
3. Income stream comparison dashboard (side-by-side ROI per platform)
4. Minimal-friction time logging (calendar inference + one-tap timer)

### Tier 2 (V1.1 — High Value)
5. Income goal tracking (minimum / target / stretch)
6. Platform fee modeling (auto-deduct Fiverr 20%, Upwork 10%, etc.)
7. Benchmark comparison layer (anonymized peer data)
8. Tax export / annual income summary

### Tier 3 (V2 — Differentiation)
9. A/B pricing experiments with statistical significance
10. Availability heatmap (best times per platform)
11. Income forecasting / projection
12. Client/platform exit recommendations

## Acquisition Channel Implications
- **r/freelance and r/sidehustle** are primary organic acquisition channels — product should natively support "show your dashboard" sharing
- "True hourly rate calculator" as a free tool drives top-of-funnel from these communities
- Tax season (January-April) is the highest-intent acquisition window for all segments
