# Shopify Analytics — Competitor Analysis

**URL:** https://www.shopify.com/  
**Analyzed:** April 2026  
**Category:** E-commerce Analytics

## Overview
- **Target User:** E-commerce store owners (dropshippers, product creators, digital sellers)
- **Pricing:** Basic $29/month, Shopify $79/month, Advanced $299/month; analytics depth scales with tier
- **Core Value Prop:** Complete e-commerce platform with built-in analytics for store performance

## Key Features
- Sales, orders, and revenue dashboards
- Conversion funnel analytics (sessions → add-to-cart → checkout → purchase)
- Product performance reports (best sellers, inventory)
- Customer lifetime value and repeat purchase rate
- Traffic sources and marketing attribution
- Finance reports (gross profit, COGS if entered)
- Abandoned cart analytics
- Google Analytics integration
- Shopify Balance (business bank account)
- Multi-channel selling (Instagram, TikTok, etc.)

## UX Patterns
- **Dashboard:** Overview with revenue, orders, sessions KPI tiles; live view of active visitors
- **Reports:** 60+ pre-built report types; date range comparisons; export all
- **Cohort Analysis:** Customer retention by acquisition date (Advanced only)
- **Attribution:** Multi-touch marketing attribution for ad spend ROI
- **Mobile:** Shopify app shows real-time sales; notification per sale (dopamine hit)

## Strengths
- Most comprehensive e-commerce analytics of any platform
- Real-time sales feed creates engagement
- Marketing attribution helps sellers optimize ad spend
- Customer LTV and repeat rate → long-term business health metrics
- Beautiful, modern UI with strong data visualization

## Weaknesses / Gaps
- **No time tracking** — no concept of owner/operator hours
- **No true hourly rate for store owner** — doesn't factor in customer service, fulfillment, content time
- **Not designed for service providers or hybrid gig workers**
- **Expensive** — $79-299/month is too much for a side hustle earning $500/month
- **No multi-platform view** — doesn't integrate Etsy, Fiverr, consulting income

## Relevance to GigAnalytics
- Shopify sellers who also do freelance work or sell on other platforms need a unified view. GigAnalytics fills this cross-platform gap.
- **Integration:** Shopify has a well-documented API; CSV export is standard.
