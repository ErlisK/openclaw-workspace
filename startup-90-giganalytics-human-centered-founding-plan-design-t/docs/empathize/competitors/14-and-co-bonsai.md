# AND.CO / Bonsai — Competitor Analysis

**URL:** https://hellobonsai.com/  
**Analyzed:** April 2026  
**Category:** All-in-One Freelance Business Management

## Overview
- **Target User:** Independent freelancers and small agencies who need contracts, invoicing, time tracking, and taxes in one place
- **Pricing:** Starter $17/month (1 user), Professional $32/month, Business $52/month (multiple users)
- **Background:** AND.CO was acquired by Fiverr in 2018 and later rebranded/evolved; Bonsai is the independent successor targeting the same market
- **Core Value Prop:** "The everything app for freelancers" — proposals, contracts, invoices, time tracking, accounting, taxes

## Key Features
- Proposal builder (beautiful client-facing docs)
- Contract templates and e-signatures (legally binding)
- Project management with task lists
- Time tracking (timers per project/task)
- Invoice generation from proposals and time entries
- Expense tracking and receipt capture
- Accounting and bookkeeping reports
- Tax estimation (quarterly estimates)
- Client CRM
- Scheduling/availability link (like Calendly built-in)
- Subcontractor management

## UX Patterns
- **Onboarding:** Choose your workflow path (proposals, invoicing, time tracking) → guided setup
- **Proposal → Contract → Invoice Pipeline:** Unified flow where proposal converts to contract, contract generates invoice template; highly opinionated workflow
- **Dashboard:** Active projects, outstanding invoices, recent time entries, upcoming tasks
- **Tax Center:** All-year tax summary, quarterly payment reminders, deduction categories

## Strengths
- Most complete end-to-end freelance workflow of any competitor
- Contract templates reduce legal anxiety significantly
- Tight proposal → payment pipeline reduces admin friction
- Tax estimation built in (rare feature done well)
- Strong onboarding — doesn't overwhelm with all features at once

## Weaknesses / Gaps
- **Assumes client-facing freelance work only** — no marketplace income model
- **No multi-platform aggregation** — Bonsai is your business, not your analytics layer
- **No competitive intelligence or peer benchmarking**
- **Pricing is high for the income level of many target users**
- **No "true hourly rate" across diverse income types**
- **No scheduling heatmap for marketplace availability**
- **No A/B pricing experiments**
- Manual input still required for most data

## UX Flows (Annotated)
1. **Proposal flow:** New Proposal → client details → service description + rate → send link → client views/accepts → auto-converts to contract → e-sign → invoice generated. Outstanding end-to-end.
2. **Time tracking:** Start timer on project → stop → time auto-appended to project's billable hours → included in next invoice. Clean integration.
3. **Tax estimate:** Transactions auto-categorized → Tax Center shows estimated quarterly payment → set reminder → pay IRS direct link. Anxiety-reducing.

## Relevance to GigAnalytics
- **Bonsai is the benchmark for "all-in-one freelance"** but serves traditional client-billing freelancers only.
- **Key Gap:** None of Bonsai's features work for a Fiverr seller, Etsy shop owner, or rideshare driver. GigAnalytics serves this underserved majority.
- **Potential integration:** GigAnalytics analytics layer sitting alongside Bonsai for users who do both client work AND marketplace work.
