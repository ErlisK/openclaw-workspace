# Proto-Persona Empathy Maps — Index

**Format:** 6-quadrant Think / Feel / See / Do / Pain / Gain  
**Date:** April 2026  
**Research basis:** 14 competitor analyses, 10 forum sources (15M+ community members), StackExchange API data, competitor product reviews

---

## Why Proto-Personas?

These are **proto-personas** — synthesized from desk research and public signals rather than primary interviews. They represent the highest-confidence composite profiles we can build before talking directly to users. They should be validated and refined in the next research phase (user interviews, surveys).

Proto-personas differ from full personas in that:
- They are explicitly labeled as research-derived (not interview-derived)
- Quotes are synthesized composites from public forum patterns, not verbatim
- Confidence levels are noted where evidence is stronger or weaker

## The Three Proto-Personas

### 🎨 Persona 1: Creator-Seller
**File:** `01-creator-seller.md`  
**Represents:** Etsy shop + Gumroad/digital products + optional Fiverr gigs  
**Core tension:** Creative identity vs. financial reality; physical products vs. digital efficiency  
**Critical insight:** Etsy physical products likely earn $6-9/hr effective; digital products likely earn $35-50/hr. She doesn't know this.  
**Primary JTBD:** "Show me which of my creative platforms is actually worth my time."  
**Killer GigAnalytics moment:** Physical vs. digital $/hr comparison → immediate reallocation of creative energy  

---

### 💼 Persona 2: Service Freelancer
**File:** `02-service-freelancer.md`  
**Represents:** Design / development / coaching / consulting — sold via Upwork + direct clients + retainers  
**Core tension:** Expertise worth + rate anxiety; billable vs. unbillable time  
**Critical insight:** Proposal time, revisions, and client management erode stated $/hr by 30-50%. Never accounted for.  
**Primary JTBD:** "Show me my true effective hourly rate per client/platform including all the time I don't bill."  
**Killer GigAnalytics moment:** Upwork effective $/hr vs. direct client $/hr → clear argument to invest in direct client pipeline  

---

### 🚗 Persona 3: Platform Gig Worker
**File:** `03-platform-gig-worker.md`  
**Represents:** Uber/Lyft + DoorDash + TaskRabbit + Rover — physical service apps  
**Core tension:** Time maximization vs. platform selection; gross earnings vs. real take-home after expenses  
**Critical insight:** Uber may net only $11/hr after gas; TaskRabbit may net $26/hr. Without data, this person defaults to Uber because it's always available.  
**Primary JTBD:** "Tell me which platform to open right now to earn the most per hour."  
**Killer GigAnalytics moment:** Platform $/hr comparison → shift from lowest-earning to highest-earning platform allocation

## Cross-Persona Comparison

| Dimension | Creator-Seller | Service Freelancer | Platform Gig Worker |
|-----------|---------------|-------------------|---------------------|
| Income range | $800–$4K/mo | $4K–$15K/mo | $800–$3.5K/mo |
| Tech comfort | Medium | Medium-High | Medium |
| Primary device | Mobile + Desktop | Desktop + Mobile | Mobile only |
| Biggest hidden cost | Production time + materials | Proposal + admin time | Gas + vehicle wear |
| Rate data gap | Per-product-type $/hr | Per-client/stream $/hr | Per-platform $/hr after expenses |
| Tax complexity | Medium (4 1099s) | High (6 sources, complex deductions) | Medium (mileage deduction missed) |
| Pricing willingness | $12–20/month | $20–35/month | $0–8/month (free tier required) |
| Acquisition channel | Etsy/creator communities | r/freelance, StackExchange, IH | r/sidehustle, gig-worker forums |
| Viral loop | Shareable income breakdown card | Rate benchmark social share | "Outearned X% of drivers in your city" |

## Shared Universal Pain (All 3 Personas)

All three proto-personas share **the same core unmet need**:

> *"I have income from multiple sources. I know roughly what I earn in gross. I don't know my true net hourly rate per source. I can't make good decisions without this data. No tool gives it to me automatically."*

This shared JTBD is GigAnalytics' single most important design target.

---

## Validation Required (Next Steps)

These proto-personas should be validated through:
1. **5-7 user interviews per persona** (Define phase)
2. **Survey distribution** to relevant Reddit/Discord communities
3. **Observation sessions** watching users do their current income tracking workflow
4. **Prototype testing** with thin MVP — see which persona converts fastest and has highest activation
