## About GrantPulse

GrantPulse is a lightweight web app that runs microgrant programs end-to-end for small community foundations, donor-advised funds (DAFs), family foundations, and local government grant programs managing 10–500 microgrants/year.

It replaces spreadsheets and email with:
- Intake forms & applicant portals
- Reviewer scoring with conflict-of-interest controls
- Milestone-based Stripe disbursements
- Templated outcome reporting
- One-click board reports & donor-facing impact dashboards

## Target Segments

| Code | Segment | Description | Typical Size |
|------|---------|-------------|--------------|
| CF | Community Foundations | Place-based foundations serving a geography | 1–5 FTE, 20–200 grants/yr |
| DAF | DAF Sponsors/Advisors | Donor-advised fund programs at community foundations or financial institutions | 1–3 staff, 10–100 grants/yr |
| FF | Family Foundations | Private family-governed foundations | 1–2 FTE (often part-time), 10–80 grants/yr |
| MO | Municipal Offices | Local government grant programs (CDBG, arts, small biz) | 1–3 FTE, 10–100 grants/yr |

## Summary: Top 5 Hypotheses to Validate First

1. **H04** — Grantee report inconsistency (highest confidence, broadest segment)
2. **H01** — Spreadsheet time drain (validates core value prop)
3. **H05** — Board report time (validates highest-value feature)
4. **H11** — Incumbent pricing too high (validates pricing strategy)
5. **H13** — Donor privacy as DAF purchase criterion (validates segment entry)
