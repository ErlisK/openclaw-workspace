## Key Excerpts

**On running it solo:**
> "I took over from the previous coordinator who quit because it was 'too much work.' She had been doing it in a notebook and Google Forms. I've modernized it to Google Sheets. I consider that a win. But it's still a lot of manual work for what is essentially 25 kids getting money to play sports."

**On the biggest pain:**
> "Every year I forget to follow up with someone. Someone submitted and I marked them as pending and then life happened and they fell through the cracks. Last year a family didn't get their scholarship because I didn't notice their application was incomplete. I found out at the end of the season. I still feel bad."

**On pricing:**
> "My budget for this is zero. But if there was a free plan that handled 25 scholarships, I'd use it in a heartbeat and tell every parent booster organization I know about it."
