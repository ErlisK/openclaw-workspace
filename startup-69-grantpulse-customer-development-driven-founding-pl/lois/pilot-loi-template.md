## Purpose

This Letter of Intent ("LOI") sets out the mutual understanding between **[Organization Name]** ("Pilot Organization") and **GrantPulse** ("GrantPulse") regarding the Pilot Organization's participation in the GrantPulse Pilot Program.

This LOI is non-binding and does not constitute a purchase commitment or formal contract. It expresses both parties' good-faith intent to participate in a time-limited pilot program for the purpose of validating GrantPulse's product and its value for small grant programs.

---

## What the Pilot Organization Commits To

By signing this LOI, the Pilot Organization agrees to:

1. **Run one full grant cycle** on GrantPulse during the pilot period (minimum: application intake through at least one disbursement and/or reporting cycle, depending on program timing).

2. **Participate in 2 structured interviews** with the GrantPulse team:
   - Pre-pilot kickoff interview (60 min): document baseline workflow, time estimates, and pain points
   - Post-pilot exit interview (60 min): share outcomes, time savings, and honest feedback

3. **Complete an exit survey** at the end of the pilot covering: time saved, feature usefulness, NPS score, and conversion interest.

4. **Provide lightweight feedback** during the pilot (1–2 Slack/email check-ins per month) to flag bugs, confusion points, or unmet needs.

5. **Introduce GrantPulse** to at least one peer organization or colleague who might benefit from the product (optional but appreciated).

---

## What GrantPulse Commits To

GrantPulse commits to:

1. **Free access** to the full GrantPulse platform for the duration of the pilot period (up to 6 months from onboarding).

2. **White-glove onboarding**: A dedicated onboarding call (90 min) to configure the organization's program, import any existing data, and train the primary user.

3. **Data export anytime**: The Pilot Organization may export all their data (applications, scores, disbursement records, reports) in standard formats (CSV, PDF) at any time and upon request, with no fees or lock-in.

4. **No lock-in**: If the Pilot Organization decides not to convert to a paid plan, GrantPulse will delete their data upon request within 30 days of pilot end.

5. **Priority support**: The GrantPulse team will respond to support requests within 1 business day during the pilot period.

6. **Honest feedback loop**: GrantPulse will share product updates and roadmap decisions influenced by pilot feedback with participating organizations.

## Pilot Success Criteria

The following criteria will be measured at the end of the pilot to evaluate success for both parties:

**For the Pilot Organization:**
- Time saved on grants admin vs. stated baseline (target: ≥20% reduction)
- At least 1 board report generated using GrantPulse
- Net Promoter Score (NPS) ≥7/10 from primary user
- Grantee applicant experience rated ≥7/10 (if survey conducted)

**For GrantPulse:**
- Pilot Organization completes ≥80% of committed activities above
- GrantPulse team receives specific, actionable feedback on at least 3 feature areas
- At least 1 testimonial or case study quote provided (optional, with org approval)

## Data Processing & Privacy

The Pilot Organization's data (applicant information, grant decisions, financial data) is:

- Stored securely in GrantPulse's cloud infrastructure (Supabase / AWS)
- Accessible only to authorized users designated by the Pilot Organization
- Not shared with third parties for any purpose
- Exportable and deletable at any time upon request

GrantPulse will execute a Data Processing Agreement (DPA) upon request. A standard DPA template is available at [grantshelf.com/legal] *(coming soon)*.

For organizations with specific GDPR, CCPA, or state-level data requirements, please flag these during onboarding.

---

## Procurement Notes

- **No billing or invoicing occurs during the pilot period.** There is no contract and no payment obligation.
- If the Pilot Organization decides to convert to a paid plan after the pilot, a formal agreement will be presented at that time.
- For organizations that require vendor approval, IT security review, or procurement documentation, GrantPulse can provide:
  - A standard vendor questionnaire response
  - Security and data handling overview document
  - Reference contacts from other pilot organizations (once available)
- Municipal or government organizations: The pilot is designed to operate under the threshold of formal procurement requirements. If procurement is required regardless, contact us at hello@grantshelf.com to discuss options.
