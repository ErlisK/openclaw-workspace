# Product Hunt Launch — ClipSpark

## Tagline
Turn any podcast or video into 5 viral clips — in 10 minutes, no editing skills needed

## Description (250 words)
**The problem:** You record great content. The best moments die inside 60-minute episodes that your audience won't watch in full. You know you should be on TikTok, Reels, and Shorts — but cutting, captioning, and formatting 5 clips per episode takes 5–10 hours you don't have.

**What ClipSpark does:**

Drop your podcast or video file. ClipSpark automatically transcribes, scores every segment across 7 quality signals, and surfaces your 5–8 best clips — complete with burned-in captions, AI-generated title variants, and hashtag suggestions.

Review and approve in a browser editor. Trim if you want. Pick a title. Publish directly to YouTube Shorts and LinkedIn, or download for TikTok and Instagram.

**The workflow:**
1. Upload (2 min)
2. Processing runs in background (~5 min for a 60-min episode)
3. Review and approve clips (10 min)
4. Publish same day

**Built for:** solo podcasters, indie creators, coaches, and founders who publish weekly and have audiences under 10k — people who can't afford a full-time video editor but know short-form content is where discovery happens.

**Pricing:**
- Free: 5 clips/month, up to 60-min episodes
- Pro ($9/mo or $50/yr): unlimited clips, 120-min episodes, HD export, direct publishing

## First Comment / Maker Note
Hey everyone! Maker here.

A few things I want to be transparent about:

**What's working well:** The heuristic scoring engine is surprisingly accurate at finding hook moments — it catches ~70-80% of what a human editor would flag as "clipworthy." The caption rendering is solid (Whisper transcription + SRT burn-in via ffmpeg).

**Known limitations:**
- You still need to do a quick review pass — the AI sometimes picks technically high-scoring segments that are contextually awkward (mid-sentence cuts, etc.)
- TikTok and Instagram are download-first right now (direct upload is blocked behind their API review process)
- No mobile app yet

**What I'm building next:** Community-shared clip templates, A/B title testing (LinkedIn + Shorts performance data back-feed), and team collaboration for podcasting agencies.
