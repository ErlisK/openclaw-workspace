## Template Marketplace Growth Plan

Target: ≥10 community templates with ≥100 uses each

### Template seeding strategy

**Phase 1 (now):** Seed 10 high-quality templates in-house. Names like:
- "Bold TikTok Hook" (karaoke, fast pace)
- "LinkedIn Insight Drop" (minimal, 60–90s)
- "Podcast Story Arc" (kinetic, 45–75s)
- "Founder Lesson" (branded)
- "Q&A Answer" (bold white, 45–60s)

**Phase 2 (month 2):** Add "Share Template" button to clip edit page. Let users save their preferred caption style + export settings as a named template.

**Phase 3 (month 3):** Community leaderboard. Templates ranked by uses. Top 3 templates get a "Community Pick" badge. Template creators get 1 credit per 10 uses.

### Viral coefficient ≥0.3 path

Current path: signup → upload → invite friends → they signup

Additional paths to build:
- Template sharing: "Made with [template name]" attribution on clip export
- Performance sharing: "My best clip this month: [result]" prompted at 30-day mark
- Referral program: already live (5 clips per activated referral)
