# GrantSnap Interview Screener
**Purpose:** Qualify interviewees for the 30-minute discovery interview  
**Metro Focus:** San Francisco Bay Area (Oakland / SF / Berkeley / East Bay)  
**Phase:** Phase 1 Discovery

---

## Screener Questions (Typeform / Google Form)

*Estimated time: 3 minutes*

---

**Q1. What best describes your role?**
- [ ] Executive Director / CEO
- [ ] Development Director / Manager
- [ ] Program Director / Manager
- [ ] Volunteer grant writer
- [ ] Board member involved in fundraising
- [ ] Contract / freelance grant writer
- [ ] Other: ___

*Qualify: All above eligible. If "Other," qualify if they write or review grant proposals.*

---

**Q2. What type of organization do you work with?**
- [ ] 501(c)(3) nonprofit
- [ ] Fiscally sponsored project (under a fiscal sponsor)
- [ ] Grassroots / unincorporated community group
- [ ] Tribal organization
- [ ] I work with multiple organizations (consultant / contractor)
- [ ] Other: ___

*Qualify: All above eligible.*

---

**Q3. Approximately what is your organization's annual operating budget?**
- [ ] Under $50,000
- [ ] $50,000 – $150,000
- [ ] $150,000 – $500,000
- [ ] $500,000 – $1 million
- [ ] Over $1 million

*Qualify: Under $1M preferred. Over $1M okay if grant team is ≤5 people.*

---

**Q4. How many people at your organization are actively involved in writing or assembling grant proposals? (include volunteers, board members, EDs)**
- [ ] Just me
- [ ] 2–3 people
- [ ] 4–5 people
- [ ] 6–10 people
- [ ] More than 10

*Qualify: 1–5 people preferred. 6–10 okay. Disqualify: >10 (too large for target segment).*

---

**Q5. Approximately how many grant applications does your organization submit per year?**
- [ ] Fewer than 5
- [ ] 5–10
- [ ] 11–20
- [ ] 21–40
- [ ] More than 40

*Qualify: 5+ applications/year preferred. Fewer than 5 okay if high frustration expressed.*

---

**Q6. What tools do you currently use to write and manage grant proposals? (check all that apply)**
- [ ] Google Docs / Google Drive
- [ ] Microsoft Word / SharePoint
- [ ] Email + attachments
- [ ] Spreadsheet (Google Sheets / Excel)
- [ ] Instrumentl
- [ ] GrantStation / Candid
- [ ] Salesforce / other CRM
- [ ] Notion / Airtable
- [ ] Other: ___

*Qualify: Prioritize Google Docs/Word/spreadsheet/email users. Disqualify: Instrumentl/Salesforce heavy users (already using dedicated tools).*

---

**Q7. What geographic area does your organization primarily serve?**
- [ ] San Francisco
- [ ] Oakland / East Bay
- [ ] Berkeley / Albany / Emeryville
- [ ] Marin / North Bay
- [ ] San Jose / South Bay
- [ ] Peninsula / South SF
- [ ] Broader Bay Area / multi-county
- [ ] Outside Bay Area

*Qualify: Bay Area preferred for Phase 1. Others welcomed for wait list.*

---

**Q8. Would you be willing to do a 30-minute Zoom interview in the next 2 weeks?**
- [ ] Yes — please reach out to schedule
- [ ] Maybe — depends on timing
- [ ] No

*Qualify: Yes or Maybe.*

---

**Q9. Email address** *(to schedule interview)*: _______________

**Q10. Anything you'd like us to know about your grant writing experience?** *(optional, open text)*

---

## Screener Scoring

| Score | Criteria | Action |
|-------|---------|--------|
| Priority 1 | ED/dev staff + ≤5 grant writers + 5–40 apps/yr + Google Docs/Word | Schedule ASAP |
| Priority 2 | Volunteer writer OR contract writer + any tool | Schedule within 2 weeks |
| Priority 3 | Budget >$1M OR dedicated dev team >5 people | Waitlist / skip |
